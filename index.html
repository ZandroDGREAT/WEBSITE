About the Game

“Mang Juan: Philippine Journey” is an interactive adventure game that highlights the rich culture and iconic landmarks of every Philippine region. Engage with unique NPCs, take on regional quizzes, and collect badges as you explore the beauty of the Philippines with Mang Juan. What are you waiting for? Begin your journey today!

Gameplay

How to Play: Interact with every NPC in the game and read their stories carefully. Each region ends with a quiz based on what you’ve learned from those NPCs. Pass the quiz to earn a badge, showing that you’ve explored and completed that region’s challenges
